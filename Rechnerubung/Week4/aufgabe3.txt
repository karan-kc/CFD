1. Ist OpenFOAM in der Lage, 2D-Simulationen durchzufu ̈hren? In den Beschreibungen der Anfangsbedingungen z.B. unter 0/p findet man die Einträge empty. Was bedeuten sie? Hinweis: OpenFOAM-Handbuch

Openfoam is not capable of running purely 2D simulations.
In order to work with 2D simulations, an ‘empty’ boundary condition is defined. It specifies a reduced dimensions case that informs the solver to not solve in the z-direction.

2. Was ist der Unterschied zwischen icoFoam, pisoFoam, simpleFoam und pimpleFoam?

icoFoam is a transient solver for incompressible and laminar flow of Newtonian fluids that uses the PISO algorithm.
pisoFoam is a transient solver for incompressible and turbulent flow of Newtonian fluids that uses the PISO algorithm.
simpleFoam is a steady state solver for incompressible and turbulent flow of Newtonian fluids that uses the SIMPLE algorithm.
pimpleFoam is a transient solver for incompressible and turbulent flow of Newtonian fluids on a moving mesh that uses the PIMPLE algorithm (a combination of the PISO and SIMPLE algorithm).

3. Welcher Löser eignet sich für die Simulation einer nicht-newtonschen Einphasenströmung
in OpenFOAM? Kann der Löser Turbulenz berücksichtigen?

pisoFoam is suitable for the simulation of Non-Newtonian single-phase flow. Yes, it can take turbulence into account.
